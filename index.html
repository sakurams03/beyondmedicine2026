Beyond Medicine: Biology Careers in STEM

Vision and Mission

Vision: To empower the next generation to discover innovative and diverse careers where biology meets science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Mission: To provide clear, engaging, and accessible information about biology-related careers across science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, while showcasing real-world opportunities, and helping students make informed decisions beyond traditional medical pathways.

About Us

Beyond Medicine: Biology Careers in STEM is designed to help students discover the wide range of exciting career paths biology offers beyond traditional medicine. By challenging the common belief that biology only leads to becoming a doctor, this platform provides clear and engaging insights into real-world opportunities across science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, highlighting essential skills and guiding students to explore how biology connects to all areas of STEM.
