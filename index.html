Biology Careers
Across STEM

A background in biology opens far more doors than most students realise. It goes far beyond medicine, powering innovation across science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Yet many students choose biology at A-Levels with the belief that medicine is the only destination. When that path doesn’t work out, it can feel as though all opportunities have disappeared. Others avoid biology entirely, assuming it only leads to hospitals or laboratories. In reality, that couldn’t be further from the truth.

Biology is a gateway to diverse careers. It’s not a single path, but a foundation for countless possibilities.

So, where can biology take you?

Here Are Four Powerful Pathways in Biology

Vision and Mission

Vision: To empower the next generation to discover innovative and diverse careers where Biology meets Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics.

Mission: To provide clear, engaging, and accessible information about biology-related careers across Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, while showcasing real world opportunities, and helping students make informed decisions beyond traditional medical pathways.

About Us

Biology Careers Across STEM is designed to help students discover the wide range of exciting career paths biology offers beyond traditional medicine. By challenging the common belief that biology only leads to becoming a doctor, this platform provides clear and engaging insights into real world opportunities across Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, highlighting essential skills and guiding students to explore how biology connects to all areas of STEM.
